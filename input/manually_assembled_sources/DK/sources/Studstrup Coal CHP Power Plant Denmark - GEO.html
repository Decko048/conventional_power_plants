Abstract

Studstrup Coal CHP Power Plant Denmark is located at Studstrup, Kalo Vig, Aarhus, North Jutland, Denmark. Location coordinates are: Latitude= 56.2504, Longitude= 10.344. This infrastructure is of TYPE Coal Power Plant with a design capacity of 750 MWe. It has 2 unit(s). The first unit was commissioned in 1984 and the last in 1985. It is operated by DONG Energy.
