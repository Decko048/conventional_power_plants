Production units

Gas turbine unit PB 6 Gas turbine unit PB 6, manufactured by Siemens, with a capacity of 53 MW, operates in a simple cycle.
Gas turbine units PB 4-5 Alstom GT11N2 GT units, with 2x114 MW output, operate in simple cycle. They are adapt to operate with fuel oil, fuel gas or the simultaneous combination of both. Operation began in the year 2000.
Gas turbine units PB 1-3 These GT units boast with reliability and fast starts. It takes them less than 15 minutes from start command to nominal power.
